Announcing Atavist Books

Editor-in-Chief and Business Lead proposed an announcement site for the announcement of our first season of titles. I made a prototype directly in html to show them what I envisioned for the page. After receiving feedback from the main stakeholders in the project I worked closely with our production lead to gather the images and text content needed. I designed the animations and scroll effects, and made the site responsive.

Twice Upon a Time

Author has already written story in a Word doc. I read it and suggested it be broken up into a card-like design. I built a few prototypes to show author and editor. After sign-off, I collaborated with the author to decide on best page breaks. I developed a series of prototypes for both imagery, typography, and sound placement. I worked closely with the editorial staff and author to improve the story cohesion. Author took photographs from his personal collection that I developed into chapter head collages. I built a rudimentary UI within our composition platform so that editorial staff could tweak audio and animation timings while I fixed cross-platform bugs. I also generated an e-book version of the story that was progressively enhanced for each major e-book reading device.

Sleep Donation Trailer

Creative Director Chip Kidd had already hired the illustrator to work on the novel cover and asked him to collaborate on the video with us. Chip provided me with a rough idea of his vision for the video teaser (“lots of circles”) and I developed a storyboard based on that. The editor-in-chief provided us with the key phrases from the book to use as teasers. The illustrator used the storyboard to design static illustrations. I animated the static illustrations and set it to sound effects. After showing it to the editor-in-chief we decided to simplify the animation and set it to music which I edited from a stock audio site.
