Derrick Schultz

  • About

    • I’m a graphic designer who works with digital products and creative technology. For the past three and a half years I’ve worked as a Lead Product Designer at The New York Times. Prior to the Times I worked for a couple groundbreaking-but-financially-struggling digital publishers: Atavist, Atavist Books, and Betterbook. I also have design experience in branding, exhibition design and printed book design. A PDF of my resume is available here.

    In addition to my design work I’ve taught design classes at CalArts, SVA, and Parsons School of Design. I also maintain a small art and technology practice and frequently show my work. I live in Brooklyn with my partner Ilana Kaplan and two cats, Pancake and Bug.
