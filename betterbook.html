    • Betterbook

    Open Air Publishing was a digital publisher focused on how-to books founded shortly after the release of the iPad. It was sold to Inkling in 2013.

    Digital Books

    All titles were produced on the Inkling platform, with additional titles published as Apple iBooks and EPUBs.

    Branding

    After its initial release under Open Air Publishing, the titles were eventually put into two separate brands. Betterbook was geared toward cooking, drinking, new pet owners and those interested in learning new crafts. Ready, Set, Baby! was for expecting parents.

    Each brand had a cover system and typographic specifications.
